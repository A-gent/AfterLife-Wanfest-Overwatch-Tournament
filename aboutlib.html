About The Author

Hello there!

My name is Nick, but my friends often call me Piers. I am a motion graphics and design artist, and upcoming programmer. Thanks for taking interest in this tournament of mine! If you're here, you're probably a PC gamer, and in that we share a kinship, but even if you aren't into PC, welcome to the big leagues!
This portal is for my new (as of 9/26/2016) tournament, dubbed the IEM (Intermediate Expo Madcap), and hosted at the new AfterLife LANs, organized by Northwest Arkansas Gaming Group. 2016 will be this project's flagship year.
